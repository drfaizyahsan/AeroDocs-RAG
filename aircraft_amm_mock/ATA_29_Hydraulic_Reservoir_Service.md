# AIRCRAFT MAINTENANCE MANUAL
## ATA 29-11-00
### HYDRAULIC RESERVOIR SERVICING

Hydraulic System:
- Green System
- Fluid Specification: Skydrol LD-4

### Servicing Procedure

1. Depressurize hydraulic system.
2. Verify accumulator pressure:
   - Less than 50 psi.

3. Remove reservoir service cap.

4. Add hydraulic fluid until:
   - Quantity indicator reads FULL.

#### Limits

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Max Fill Pressure | 35 psi |
| Fluid Temperature | 10°C - 45°C |

5. Install service cap.

Torque:
- Service cap: 12 Nm

6. Pressurize system and verify:
   - No leaks
   - Normal reservoir indication
