# AIRCRAFT MAINTENANCE MANUAL
## ATA 21-51-00
### AIR CONDITIONING PACK REMOVAL/INSTALLATION

### 1. General

This procedure provides instructions for removal and installation of
the Air Conditioning Pack Assembly installed in the lower fuselage
equipment bay.

Aircraft Effectivity:
- Model: A320-200 Series
- Configuration: CFM56 Powered Aircraft

### 2. References

- AMM 21-00-00: Air Conditioning System Description
- AMM 24-22-00: Electrical Power Isolation
- IPC 21-51-01: Air Conditioning Pack Assembly

### 3. Job Set-Up

#### A. Tools Required

| Tool Number | Description |
|-------------|-------------|
| STD-1024 | Torque Wrench, 20-200 Nm |
| STD-2056 | Hydraulic Lift Platform |
| TEST-311 | Pneumatic Leak Detector |

#### B. Safety Precautions

WARNING:
ISOLATE ALL ELECTRICAL POWER BEFORE BEGINNING MAINTENANCE ACTION.
FAILURE TO COMPLY MAY RESULT IN ELECTRICAL SHOCK.

CAUTION:
ALLOW THE PACK ASSEMBLY TO COOL FOR A MINIMUM OF 45 MINUTES.

### 4. Procedure

1. Open access panel 315AB.
2. Install maintenance safety locks.
3. Disconnect electrical connectors:
   - CN-A21-PWR
   - CN-A21-CONTROL
4. Disconnect pneumatic duct couplings.
5. Remove six mounting bolts.

Torque Values:
- Installation torque: 85 ±5 Nm

6. Lower pack assembly using hydraulic lift.
7. Inspect mounting brackets for corrosion and cracks.

### 5. Close-Up

Perform operational test in accordance with AMM 21-51-00-710-801.
