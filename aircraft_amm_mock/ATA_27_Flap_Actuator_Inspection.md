# AIRCRAFT MAINTENANCE MANUAL
## ATA 27-51-32
### FLAP POWER DRIVE UNIT INSPECTION

### Scheduled Maintenance Task
Task Number: 27-51-32-220-801
Interval: 12,000 Flight Hours

### Procedure

#### Visual Inspection

Inspect the flap power drive unit for:

- Hydraulic leakage
- Safety wire integrity
- Mounting bolt security
- Corrosion products
- Mechanical deformation

#### Gearbox Inspection

Measure backlash using dial indicator.

Acceptable Limits:

| Parameter | Minimum | Maximum |
|-----------|---------|---------|
| Backlash | 0.05 mm | 0.30 mm |

#### Lubrication

Lubricant:
- Aeroshell Grease 33

Application quantity:
- 18 ±2 grams

#### Rejection Criteria

Replace actuator if any of the following are observed:

- Crack indication exceeding 1.5 mm
- Gear tooth pitting >10%
- Excessive backlash
- Bearing seizure
