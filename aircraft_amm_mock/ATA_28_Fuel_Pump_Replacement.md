# AIRCRAFT MAINTENANCE MANUAL
## ATA 28-22-41
### CENTER TANK BOOST PUMP REPLACEMENT

### Hazardous Material Notice

WARNING:
FUEL VAPORS ARE FLAMMABLE.
ENSURE ALL IGNITION SOURCES ARE REMOVED.

### Preparation

1. Defuel center tank.
2. Ground aircraft.
3. Verify fuel quantity indication:
   - Left tank: <500 kg
   - Right tank: <500 kg
   - Center tank: 0 kg

### Removal

1. Open access panel 528BB.
2. Disconnect cannon plug P28-41.
3. Remove retaining fasteners.
4. Extract boost pump vertically.

### Inspection

Inspect:
- Pump impeller condition
- Electrical connector pins
- O-ring sealing surface

### Installation

Install new seal:
Part Number: MS29513-345

Torque:
- Fasteners: 42 ±3 Nm

Perform leak test after installation.
